About KREDO®
"KREDO® is not just clothing, it's belief."

Born from the intersection of silence and luxury, KREDO® was founded on a single principle: that true wealth does not announce itself. It is felt.

Every piece in the KREDO® collection is crafted with obsessive attention to material, silhouette, and intention. We produce in limited quantities because scarcity is not a marketing strategy — it is a philosophy.

We do not follow trends. We do not chase seasons. We create garments that exist outside of time — pieces that will mean as much to you in ten years as they do the day they arrive.

KREDO® is for those who understand that the most powerful statement is restraint. The loudest voice in the room is often the one that says nothing at all.

"Silence Speaks Wealth."

Fit & Measurements
Size Guide
All KREDO® garments are cut for an intentional fit. Measure yourself accurately and compare to our charts below. When in between sizes, we recommend sizing up for our oversized pieces and staying true to size for structured garments.
How to Measure
📏
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal under your arms.
📐
Shoulders
Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the other across the back, seam to seam.
📋
Length
Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to the hem of the garment.
🔄
Sleeve
Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the cuff with your arm slightly bent.

Fit Guide
Oversized Fit
VOID Tee, TRACE Tee, SILENCE Hoodie, GHOST Hoodie.
These are designed 1–2 sizes larger than standard. If you prefer a true oversized look, stay true to size. For a more fitted oversized, size down.
Regular / Boxy Fit
MUTED Shirt, ZERO Shirt, BLANK Polo.
These fit true to size with a relaxed, boxy silhouette. Measure your chest and choose accordingly.
Bottoms
NULL Cargo. Measure your waist and hips. Waist: S=28–30", M=30–32", L=32–34", XL=34–36". The cargo features an adjustable drawstring waist.
